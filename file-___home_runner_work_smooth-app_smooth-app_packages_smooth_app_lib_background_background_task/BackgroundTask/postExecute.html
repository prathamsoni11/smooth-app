To be executed after the actual run.

Mostly, cleans the temporary data changes performed in preExecute. success indicates (if true) that so far the operation was a success. With that bool we're able to deal with 2 cases:

  1. everything is fine and we may have to do something more than cleaning
  2. something bad happened and we just need to clear the task
